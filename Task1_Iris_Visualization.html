Scatter Plot¶

We will plot sepal length vs petal length to see their relationship.

In [7]:
# Scatter plot to visualize relationship between sepal length and petal length
 
plt.scatter(df['sepal_length'], df['petal_length'])
plt.xlabel("Sepal Length")  # X-axis label
plt.ylabel("Petal Length")  # Y-axis label
plt.title("Sepal vs Petal Length")  # Plot title
plt.show()

Conclusion¶

  • Sepal length and petal length show a positive relationship.
  • Most values are concentrated in a specific range.
  • A few outliers are present in the dataset.
